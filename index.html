Meet Vardan

Hello! I'm Vardan, a professional mehndi artist based in the heart of Mumbai. For me, mehndi is more than just an art form; it's a celebration of culture, beauty, and life's most joyous moments. I fell in love with this ancient tradition as a child and have spent over a decade perfecting my craft.

I use only 100% natural and organic henna paste, ensuring a rich, dark stain and a safe experience for your skin. My passion is to create unique designs that reflect your personality and make your special day even more memorable.
