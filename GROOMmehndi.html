Mehndi for the Groom

A touch of tradition for the groom. Simple, symbolic, and stylish designs to celebrate his special day.

A Modern Tradition

Mehndi isn't just for the bride! It's a beautiful tradition for the groom to partake in the celebrations with a touch of henna. Groom mehndi is typically more subtle and symbolic than bridal designs.

Popular choices include small, meaningful motifs, the bride's initials hidden in a simple pattern, or geometric designs that reflect strength and partnership. It's a wonderful way to honor tradition and add another layer of celebration to the wedding festivities.
