Arabic Style Mehndi

Characterized by its bold, vine-like patterns and beautiful use of empty space, Arabic mehndi is a timeless choice for any celebration.

The Essence of Arabic Design

Unlike traditional Indian mehndi that often covers the entire hand, Arabic designs typically feature a single, flowing pattern. This "bail" or vine usually runs diagonally from a finger down to the wrist or arm.

The motifs, such as flowers, leaves, and paisleys, are generally larger and more spaced out. This contrast between the dark henna and the bare skin makes the design incredibly striking and elegant. It's a perfect choice for sangeets, parties, and festivals.
