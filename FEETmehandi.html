The Tradition of Foot Mehndi

Applying mehndi to the feet, or 'Aalta', is a cherished tradition in Indian culture, especially for brides. It is believed to bring good fortune and protect against evil. The intricate patterns are not just decorative; they symbolize joy, beauty, and the celebration of a new beginning.

From elaborate patterns that cover the entire foot to simpler, elegant designs that resemble anklets, foot mehndi adds a final touch of grace to any festive or bridal attire. We can create designs that perfectly match the mehndi on your hands.
